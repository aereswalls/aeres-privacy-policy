Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. Do I need an internet connection to use Aeres Wallpapers?

Yes. A stable internet connection is required to use the app effectively. All wallpapers are hosted in the cloud and are dynamically loaded. Without internet access, wallpaper previews and downloads will not function properly.

2. Are the wallpapers free to use?

Yes! All wallpapers available in Aeres Wallpapers are free to use. They are sourced from public domain or licensed under free-to-use terms, making them safe for personal use on your devices.

3. Can I use the wallpapers for commercial purposes?

Most wallpapers are intended for personal use only. While many are license-free, we recommend not using them for commercial purposes unless explicitly stated.

4. How often are new wallpapers added?

New wallpapers are added regularly. You can check the “Recent” section inside the app to find the latest additions.

5. What do I do if wallpapers are not loading?

  • Ensure you have a stable internet connection (Wi-Fi or mobile data).
  • Try restarting the app or clearing cache.
  • Make sure the app is updated to the latest version available on the Play Store.

6. Are there any ads in the app?

Yes, Aeres Wallpapers displays non-intrusive ads to support development. However, you can permanently remove ads by purchasing the ad-free upgrade in the app settings.

7. How do I contact the developer?

You can contact us through the Support section inside the app or directly via email at aereslabsdev@gmail.com.

8. Where can I follow updates or report bugs?

You can follow the project or report issues through our official GitHub page or contact support from the app.

Thank you for using Aeres Wallpapers ✨
