Privacy Policy of Aeres Wallpapers

Last updated: May 29, 2025

This application ("Aeres Wallpapers") displays advertisements provided by third parties (e.g., Google AdMob) and does not directly collect any personally identifiable information.

Data collected by third parties

Third parties such as Google AdMob may automatically collect non-personal information, including:

  • Device type and operating system
  • Interactions with ads
  • Anonymous advertising identifiers (e.g., Google Advertising ID)

For more information about Google's practices, please visit the official page.

Consent for EU users

The app may request your consent for personalized advertising based on your geographic area, in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

Withdrawing consent

You can modify or withdraw your consent regarding personalized advertising at any time.

To do so, open the app, go to the Settings menu, and select "Manage privacy preferences", where you can access the consent form again.

In-app purchases

The app offers optional in-app purchases, including the ability to remove advertisements. These purchases are managed via Google Play and do not involve any personal data collection on our part.

Exclusive features for Italian users

The app may display additional features or options exclusively to users with their device language set to Italian. These features are not available or visible to users with other language settings.

Specifically, Italian users may see an option to join a Telegram channel that shares curated offers on technology products available on Amazon.it. This feature does not collect or track any personal data from the user, and it is shown only based on the device's system language ("it").

External links

The app may contain links to external services (e.g., Telegram). We are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of such services. In the case of the Telegram channel for Amazon offers, please refer to the privacy policy of Telegram and Amazon for any data processing or tracking done outside the app environment.

Contact
